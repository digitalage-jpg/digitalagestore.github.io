POLICIES

Return and Exchange policy?

Currently, we are not taking any returns. We make sure to thoroughly quality check your items before they’re packaged and sent.
Due to current COVID-19 circumstances, we only accept exchanges for defective items upon arrival. Please make sure to read the specific sizing charts under each item before placing your order.
We will only offer exchanges up to 14 days after delivery and adjustments if needed. If you'd like to make an adjustment or exchange, the product must be in original condition and no proof of alteration. Return shipping will be covered by the recipient.
To start a exchange, you can contact us at digitalage.jpg@gmail.com. You will be responsible for return shipping costs. Items sent back to us without first requesting an exchange will not be accepted.
Thank you so much for supporting us!!

Do you accept orders placed outside of the US?

Yes! Visit our online store and place an order.

How long will it take to receive my order?

Once shipped most domestic orders take anywhere from 2 to 5 business days to arrive. International shipping delivery times may vary between 2 and 12 weeks due to COVID 19 circumstances.
Please allow up 48 hours for processing after you’ve placed your order. You will receive a confirmation email from us stating we have received your order.
Please allow up to 72 hours after you've received a confirmation email for your tracking and shipment information.

Where can I see more photos of your jewelry?

Hi! We are just two Asian-American boys out here. We post everything to our Instagram page or you can check out our founders @daryn.wn and @hyuntaees

Damages or Issues?

Please inspect your order upon reception and contact us immediately if the item is defective, damaged or if you receive the wrong item, so that we can evaluate the issue and make it right.
